About Me

Hello, I'm Salma Hassan

With a versatile, charismatic, and confident voice, I bring your message to life, connecting genuinely with a global audience, whatever the tone or context — and to put a smile on your face when you hear the final project.

Let's get started — I'm excited to work with you!

Hello! Thanks for stopping by. Let me share a little about my journey in Voice Over.

For me, voiceover has never been just a job — it's been a passion and obsession since childhood. I practiced everywhere: at home, at school, on the street talking to myself, imagining reading ad scripts, or being a news anchor.

Over the years, I took deliberate steps to turn my passion into a profession. Despite challenges, in 2016 I finally made it — my passion became my full-time career, and I began my journey as a professional voiceover artist.

Since then, I've worked on a wide range of projects, including advertisements, audiobooks, IVR, animation, e-learning, and more. I've had the honor of collaborating with major international and local brands — and the journey continues!

When we collaborate, you can trust that every project is crafted with passion and love, because what we do with heart is always the best.
